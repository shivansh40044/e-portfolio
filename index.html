5. Movie Review

Movie Title: 3 idots

Review: 3 Idiots is one of those movies that stays with you long after it ends. It’s not just about three engineering students—it’s about the pressure so many of us feel to meet expectations, chase grades, and follow paths that aren’t ours. What really touched me was how Rancho, the main character, questioned the system and inspired others to learn for the sake of understanding, not just for exams. The mix of emotions, humor, and strong messages makes this film relatable, especially for students like me.

Related Questions & Answers

  • Q1: Q1: What did the movie teach you?
    A:It taught me to focus on learning what I truly enjoy, instead of just trying to impress others or blindly follow rules.
  • Q2: Who did you relate to the most?
    A:I related most to Farhan—he struggled between doing what his parents wanted and following his own dreams, something many of us face.
  • Q3: Would you watch it again? Why?
    A: Definitely. It’s not just entertaining—it reminds me to stay true to myself and not be afraid to take a different path.
